Welcome to Keepitsimple

This task management tool is the easiest way to manage your todo lists for all of your projects. Responsive and ergonomic, one screen is enough to handle task creation, modification, deletion. Tasks can have different status (red, orange, green, gray and yellow), can be ordered, and are searchable.

Technologies

keepitsimple is an AJAX-ed application developed on top of JQuery and bootstrap.
